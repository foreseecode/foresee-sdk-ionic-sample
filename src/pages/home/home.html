ForeSee Ionic In Session Sample
This sample demonstrates the In Session type of invitation.

In this example, Significant Events are used to qualify for eligibility for the invitation. The threshold is set to 3. Tap the Increment SE button 3 times to meet eligibility.

Tapping the Check Eligibility button will trigger an eligibility check. If our significant event is at or past our threshold, the invite will present.

Once the invite is shown, the SDK drops into an idle state until the repeat days have elapsed. Click Reset State to reset the state of the SDK.
